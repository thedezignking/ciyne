# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Professionals and freelancers — contractors, consultants, and creatives who need to sign contracts and agreements quickly without being forced into an enterprise tool or subscription. They're mid-task: a document arrived, they need to sign it, they want to move on. Speed and simplicity matter more than features.

## Product Purpose

Ciyne is a free, browser-based PDF signing tool. Upload a PDF, upload a signature photo, drag it into position, download the signed document. No account, no watermark, no quality loss. Success means a signed PDF in under two minutes.

## Brand Personality

Warm, direct, capable. Like a knowledgeable friend who helps you get something done — not a corporate tool, not a toy. The no-account, no-watermark promise is the brand voice, not just a feature bullet.

## Anti-references

- DocuSign / Adobe Sign: corporate blue, heavy onboarding, enterprise weight
- Bubbly SaaS startups: purple gradients, excessive rounding, "supercharge your workflow" copy
- Sterile minimal: all white, no personality, feels like a forgotten government form
- Fintech dashboards: dark mode data chrome, trading terminal aesthetic

## Design Principles

1. **Get out of the way**: Three steps. Each step has one clear next action. Never make the user think.
2. **Trustworthy without being corporate**: Warm and grounded, not stuffy or cold.
3. **Earn every element**: No decoration that doesn't work. No complexity for its own sake.
4. **Privacy as personality**: The no-account promise shows up in the interface, not just the tagline.
5. **Capable defaults**: Smart behavior out of the box. Configuration is a last resort.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. All interactive elements keyboard-accessible. Visible focus states on all controls. Error messages specific and actionable. Reduced motion respected.
